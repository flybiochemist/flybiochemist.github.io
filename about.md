I am a Postdoctoral Associate in the [Zoghbi Lab](https://www.bcm.edu/research/labs/huda-zoghbi) at Baylor College of Medicine, working under the mentorship of Dr. Huda Zoghbi. My research focuses on understanding the mechanisms underlying Parkinson's disease using the *Snca*<sup>G51D/G51D</sup> knock-in mouse model, with a particular emphasis on neuron-glia interactions mediated through metabolite exchanges.
